2.BATTLE OF KANWAH(1526)

It was this time BABUR vs RANA SANGRAM SINGH. Babur won this battle against Rajput king of Mewar Rana sangram singh.

RESULTS OF BATTLE OF KANWAH:

For the first time, Babur declared JIHAD (holowar) on Rajput’s. The battle consolidated the Mughal Empire foundation stone.
